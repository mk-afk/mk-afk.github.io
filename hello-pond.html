hello, pond

Jun 12, 2026 · meta canvas

This site needed a reason to exist beyond holding a resume, so I gave it one: a pond. Everything swimming on the home page is procedural. No sprites, no video files, just a canvas and some geometry.

Each koi is a chain of 26 points. The head steers, and every point behind it follows the one ahead at a fixed distance. That constraint is the entire skeleton, three lines:

const d = Math.hypot(dx, dy) || 1;
cx[i] = cx[i-1] + (dx / d) * segLen;
cy[i] = cy[i-1] + (dy / d) * segLen;

A sine wave travels down the spine to make it swim, the body is an outline drawn at varying widths along those points, and depth is faked by tinting everything toward the water color. The markings are random blobs fixed per fish, so every visitor gets koi nobody else has seen.

I'll write here occasionally about whatever I'm building or reading. No schedule. The fish don't keep one either.
